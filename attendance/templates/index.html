About Us

Shaping the Future of Technology

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering is one of the flagship departments of our institution, established with a commitment to academic excellence and holistic development of students. We offer state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and industry-aligned curriculum.

Our programs are designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements, equipping students with both theoretical knowledge and hands-on experience in modern technologies including Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, and Cybersecurity.

Our Pillars

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized centre of excellence in Computer Science and Engineering education and research, producing highly competent graduates who contribute meaningfully to society and the technology industry. We strive to foster a culture of innovation, ethical practice, and lifelong learning.

Our Mission

  • Provide quality education through innovative teaching-learning methodologies
  • Foster research, entrepreneurship, and technological innovation among students
  • Build strong industry-academia partnerships for real-world exposure
  • Develop ethical, socially responsible computing professionals
  • Ensure career readiness through holistic personality development
