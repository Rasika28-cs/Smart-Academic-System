About the Department

Building the Next Generation of Cyber Professionals

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (Cyber Security) provides students with a strong foundation in computing alongside specialized expertise in modern cyber security technologies.

The curriculum covers Ethical Hacking, Digital Forensics, Cryptography, Network Security, Web Security, and Secure Software Development, preparing students for emerging industry challenges.
